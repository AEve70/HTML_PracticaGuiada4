Angela's Recipe for the best Cinnamon Roll

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspon yeast
  • ¼ granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with a plastic bag.Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours
